Privacy Policy

Welcome to PlayCoreDrive.com. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through this Privacy Policy. This document outlines what information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to protect it.

Our platform uses third-party services (such as booking systems and analytics tools) to enhance user experience. These services may collect anonymous usage data such as browser type, device information, IP address, and website interactions.

Any information collected is used strictly for:

We may also integrate with trusted third-party APIs for hotel booking, event scheduling, and payment processing. These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Although we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
